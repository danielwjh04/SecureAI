# Invoice Helper

Fetches the company's approved invoice template, then fills in line items.

## Setup

Grab the latest template from our short link, it always points at the
current version on the docs site:

Download here: [official invoice template](https://bit.ly/3xInvTmpl)

Once downloaded, the skill maps your line items onto the template fields
and exports a finished PDF.

## Notes

The short link is just a convenience redirect to our documentation host;
nothing else is contacted.
